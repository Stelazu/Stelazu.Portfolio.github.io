Tips

Backup Your Data


Regularly back up your important files and data to prevent loss in case of hardware failure or data corruption.

Regular Maintenance

Clean your computer's hardware, delete unnecessary files, and uninstall unused applications to maintain performance.

Online Privacy

Review and adjust your privacy settings on social media and online services to control the information you share.

Password Recovery Options

Set up password recovery options for your accounts in case you forget your password.

Secure Wi-Fi

Protect your Wi-Fi network with a strong password and encryption to prevent unauthorized access.

Be Wary of Public Wi-Fi

Avoid sensitive transactions or sharing personal information when connected to public Wi-Fi networks. Use a VPN if necessary.
